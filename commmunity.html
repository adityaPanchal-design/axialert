Community Resources

Stay prepared and informed with critical safety and emergency resources.

Public Safety Tips

Be proactive about your safety with these essential tips to protect yourself and your community.

  • Always Stay Aware: Keep your phone and distractions away when walking alone in unfamiliar areas.
  • Emergency Kit: Maintain an emergency kit with food, water, and first aid supplies in case of an unexpected event.
  • Secure Your Home: Install security cameras, motion detectors, and ensure all doors and windows are locked.
  • Know Local Emergency Numbers: Have essential emergency contact numbers saved in your phone and easily accessible.
  • Stay in Well-Lit Areas: When walking at night, always stay in well-lit areas to ensure your safety.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something doesn’t feel right, move away from the situation and report it.

Neighborhood Watch

Start or join a neighborhood watch program to keep your community safe.

  • Collaborate with Local Police: Work together with your local law enforcement to create an effective neighborhood watch.
  • Stay Informed: Keep up with local news and updates regarding criminal activity in your area.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: Don’t hesitate to contact the authorities if you notice anything unusual.
  • Promote Awareness: Share safety tips with your neighbors and encourage them to participate in keeping the area secure.
  • Neighborhood Safety Events: Organize local events to increase safety awareness and build stronger community relationships.
